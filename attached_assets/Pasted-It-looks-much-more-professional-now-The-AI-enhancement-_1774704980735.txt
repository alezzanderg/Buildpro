**SCOPE OF WORK**

That means your PDF template is probably printing the section title automatically, while the AI content is also returning the title inside the section body. You should choose one or the other, not both.

If your goal is that “Enhance with AI” should only polish existing text, then the current prompt is still allowing too much expansion.
